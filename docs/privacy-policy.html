Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

TapRVU collects only the minimum information needed to provide app features, including account identity, subscription status, and data you create (such as logs and templates).

Data may be stored locally on your device and, when enabled, synced to your private iCloud container.

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. TapRVU does not receive full payment credentials.

You can export data and manage account-related actions inside app settings.
